Frequently asked questions

Is this suitable for kids born abroad?

Yes — the program is designed specifically for children growing up outside India. Stories are in English with a few Marathi/Hindi words to keep cultural flavour.

Do parents need to attend?

Parents are welcome to join, but sessions are child-focused. We provide take-home activities so parents can continue the conversation at home.

What language are stories conducted in?

Predominantly English, with simple Marathi/Hindi phrases and shlokas taught with meanings and actions.

Why are prices not listed?

Each family’s needs are unique. We discuss the best fit during a short consultation to personalise the experience.
